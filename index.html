Instructions

  1. To Play The Game, Keep Your Laptop Straight.
  2. Next Sit 3-4 Feet Away From The Laptop.
  3. After That, You Have to Move Your Right Wrist in a Ascending or Descending order.
  4. Then You Press the Play Button to Play the Game.
  5. The Red Paddle is the Your Paddle
